Impact on Developer Time Allocation

AI augmentation significantly shifts how developers spend their time, automating repetitive tasks and freeing up more capacity for high-value strategic work like system design and planning.

Platform Engineering Comes of Age

Platform Engineering is now a standard enterprise practice focused on creating a seamless "golden path" for developers. This reduces friction and cognitive load, enabling development teams to deliver value faster and more reliably.

Key Focus: Developer Experience (DevEx)

The core goal is to improve the developer experience by abstracting away the complexities of underlying infrastructure. Managed Internal Developer Platforms (IDPs) and new DevEx metrics are making this more achievable for companies of all sizes.

Managed IDPs

Cloud providers now offer out-of-the-box developer portals, democratizing access to elite-level tooling.

DevEx Metrics

Tools that quantify developer friction points are now used to guide platform improvements effectively.

AI Toolchain Integration

Platforms now provide streamlined and secure access to LLMs, vector DBs, and LLMOps frameworks.

VEX Impact on Vulnerability Alerts

The adoption of the Vulnerability Exploitability eXchange (VEX) standard dramatically reduces alert fatigue, allowing teams to focus only on vulnerabilities that are confirmed to be exploitable in their code.
